Valuable Insights

Finding out how much an industry or job role is paying is no easy task. Yes, there are other platforms to help users find out what a job is paying however, these other sites are frequently inaccurate, difficult to understand, and hard to compare to other jobs. They require a tedious sign-in process, and you cannot bookmark jobs easily to track how industries have changed over time. Upgrade your account to access our predictive modeling so you can better understand the past, present, and future of the industry. Super Loud does the heavy lifting of data scrubbing and analytics so you can just focus on the right information.

Reliable Information

Super Loud strives to get our users only the most accurate data. We double, triple, and quadruple check our numbers. Not even Santa does that. For our analytics, we only publish our findings when we have a high degree of confidence. That means in the upgraded tier, of our 400K available forecasts (with a goal of 1MM models by the end of 2022), we have made sure they pass an acceptable margin of error during their back-testing stage. If they failed during validation, we review the reason why and try to remedy the issue. Super Loud adopts the "garbage in, garbage out" mentality and wants to make sure our users can trust our data.

Simple Management

Need to manage multiple users? Done. Need multiple admins? Done. Need to delete a user? Done. Need to see all past transactions, upcoming bills, and user changes in your account? Done. Need to delete your account without having to call customer support and listen to crackly elevator music for an hour? Done. Doing tedious admin work isn't the point of our platform, so let's leave that out of the picture and keep it simple. Our Users and Transaction pages have all the information you need to make sure there are no surprises.
